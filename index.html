ABOUT TECH TWISTER

Tech twister is an Annual Inter-Collegiate technical event organized by Shree Rayeshwar Institute of Engineering & Information Technology, Shiroda. The objective is to bring together the students of different institutes on a common platform where they are encouraged to share knowledge and innovative ideas by instilling the ‘think out of the box’ attitude. It is an amalgamation of fun and learning where spectacular ideas & talent is showcased and the students learn and feel inspired. It aims to exhibit & hone communication skills as well as technical skills of the aspiring students also guiding them to dream big & achieve bigger.

ABOUT TECHTWISTER

TechTwister is an annual technical event organized by Shree Rayeshwar Institute of Engineering and Information Technology. This Event is been organized by the Institution every year in order to provide a common platform for students from different colleges to showcase their talent in the field of technology. TechTwister also encourages the students to come up with their innovative ideas and show their excellences in the field of technology. Techtwister'23 has come up with even more interesting and thrilling events this year.
